Baked Pineapple Casserole

Description

This baked pineapple casserole made with bread, crushed pineapple, eggs, and spices goes nicely with a ham dinner

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Pineapple with juice
  • Cinnamon
  • White bread

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Beat sugar and butter in a mixing bowl with an electric mixer until creamy. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in cinnamon and nutmeg. Mix in crushed pineapple and bread until incorporated; transfer to the prepared baking dish
  3. Bake
